Essendar - An online co-op platformer

Essendar was my finals project that I made together with another student. The characters in game both represent us. The name Essendar was made by using the initials of both of our first names. Stijn & Robin -> S & R -> Essendar

It's an online co-op platformer where the players need to work together in order to advance. Stijn & Robin get pulled into the game world of Essendar and during this process they end up accidentally killing the actual heroes of the world. This leaves them with a choice to make, either they stay in the game world and become the new heroes or they try and find a way to leave the game world, but in doing so they would leave the game world defenseless.

Ocarina Player

A project for my embedded system course. Connecting a N64 controller to an arduino to play notes, these notes get picked up by the microphone and analyzed based on their tone frequency. The game then displays the note you played based on the frequency. For convenience it's also possible to use keyboard input to play the notes using the arrowkeys and A.

I'm a big Zelda fan and I really love the music of the series so making this was tons of fun.
